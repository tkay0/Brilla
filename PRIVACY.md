# Privacy Policy for Brilla

**Last Updated:** July 21, 2026

Welcome to **Brilla**, an NSMQ practice application designed to help students prepare for National Science and Maths Quiz competitions.

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

---

## Information We Collect

When you create an account, we may collect:

- Email address or phone number
- School name
- Profile picture (optional)

We also collect basic account information necessary to provide and maintain your account.

---

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Create and manage your account
- Authenticate your identity when you sign in
- Personalize your experience
- Display your profile information within the app
- Improve the functionality, security, and reliability of Brilla

---

## Profile Pictures

Uploading a profile picture is optional.

If you choose to upload one, it will be stored securely and displayed as part of your profile within the application.

---

## Data Storage

Your account information is stored securely using infrastructure hosted on **DigitalOcean** and a **Neon PostgreSQL** database.

We take reasonable measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

---

## Sharing Your Information

We do **not** sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

We may disclose your information only when:

- Required by law.
- Necessary to protect the security, rights, or integrity of Brilla and its users.

---

## Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account remains active.

If you request deletion of your account, we will remove or anonymize your personal information within a reasonable period unless we are legally required to retain certain information.

---

## Your Rights

Depending on applicable laws, you may have the right to:

- Access your personal information.
- Update or correct your information.
- Request deletion of your account and personal data.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the email below.

---

## Children's Privacy

Brilla is intended for students preparing for academic competitions.

We do not knowingly collect personal information in violation of applicable laws protecting children. If you believe that such information has been collected, please contact us so that we can take appropriate action.

---

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

Any changes will be posted on this page, and the **Last Updated** date at the top will be revised accordingly.
